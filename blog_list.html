In Transit Through Timeless Lands: A Backpacker’s Odyssey Through North India’s Spirit

A traveller and a tourist walk different paths. A tourist clings to comfort, cocooned in luxury hotels and air-conditioned cabs, ticking off landmarks like a checklist. A traveller, though, dives into the raw pulse of a place—low-budget, local buses, crowded trains, and roadside stalls, chasing experiences over ease. They study a place’s soul, its history, its people, knowing that memories forged in the chaos outlast any five-star suite. Not everyone can chase this dream; life’s demands often anchor us.
